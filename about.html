About Us

Anchored on KwaZulu-Natal’s picturesque; Pine City isideally positioned at the Shelly Beach and Izotsha Rd off-ramp from the N2 highway, Pine City Mall is as easy as one, two, sea.Our bright and spacious centre boasts over 60 stores in a secure and homely atmosphere. Perfectly suited to families, we provide both locals and holidaymakers with a relaxed environment where they can enjoy some of the best shopping Pine City has to offer.

Our ocean-fresh assortment of reputable national retailers, complemented by our selection of stunning line stores, have been handpicked to suit our community’s every need. These stores range from fashion, health and beauty, to home and décor, restaurants and more.

Accessibility: Pine City Mall’s extensive parking lot means there is ample parking available. Disabled parking and allocated parking for pregnant women is also provided and we offer wheelchairs should shoppers require assistance. We also have family and disabled bathrooms.
