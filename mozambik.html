MOZAMBIK

With its roots firmly set in passion and its flavours unapologetically Afro Porto, the Mozambik restaurant group is fast becoming one of South Africa’s leading dining brands. From its launch as a single store in Ballito, Kwa-Zulu Natal circa 17 years ago, the brand has seen rapid growth over the past half-decade, now totaling 28 stores nationally, and launched it’s first international store in Lusaka, Zambia.

RECOGNITION

Over time the brand has received several accolades from a host of positive media and social influencer reviews. On average Mozambik stores are scored at four stars by its customers and several TripAdvisor 5-star certifications (2015-18). Mozambik also achieved pole position in various published restaurant rankings across South Africa.
